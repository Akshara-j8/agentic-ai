# Account Security Policy

## Purpose

This policy protects customer accounts and limits changes when identity is uncertain.

## Clauses

**AS-1 Verification Required.** Agents must verify the customer before discussing order details, issuing credits, changing addresses, or modifying account information.

**AS-2 Acceptable Verification.** Acceptable verification includes matching the account email plus one of: order ID, billing ZIP code, last four digits of the payment method, or recent shipment address.

**AS-3 Failed Verification.** If verification fails, agents may provide only general policy information and must not confirm whether an account or order exists.

**AS-4 Address Changes.** Shipping address changes after fulfillment require carrier intercept availability or supervisor approval.

**AS-5 Payment Data.** Agents must never ask for full card numbers, passwords, one-time passcodes, or security answers in chat.

**AS-6 Account Takeover Risk.** Escalate immediately if the customer reports unauthorized account access, unfamiliar orders, changed email, or suspicious password reset activity.

**AS-7 Audit Notes.** Case notes should record the verification method used, but must not include sensitive authentication details.
