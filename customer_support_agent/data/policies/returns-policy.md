# Returns Policy

## Purpose

This policy defines return eligibility and agent responsibilities for physical goods.

## Clauses

**RT-1 Return Window.** Most items may be returned within 30 days of delivery if they are unused, complete, and in original packaging.

**RT-2 Damaged Items.** Damaged-on-arrival items may be returned or replaced when the customer provides a description and, when practical, a photo.

**RT-3 Non-Returnable Items.** Final-sale, personalized, perishable, and hygiene-sensitive items are not returnable unless damaged or legally required.

**RT-4 Return Shipping.** The company pays return shipping when the return is caused by damage, wrong item, duplicate shipment, or confirmed fulfillment error.

**RT-5 Customer Preference Returns.** Customers are responsible for return shipping when the item matches the listing and the return is due to preference, size, or changed mind.

**RT-6 Inspection.** Refunds for returned goods may be held until the warehouse confirms receipt and condition.

**RT-7 Exceptions.** Agents must escalate requests outside the return window unless the customer cites a legal right, accessibility need, or prior written promise.
