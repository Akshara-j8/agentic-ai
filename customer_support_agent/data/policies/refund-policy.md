# Refund Policy

## Purpose

This policy defines when support may issue refunds, reversals, or goodwill credits without additional approval.

## Clauses

**RF-1 Eligibility.** Customers may receive a refund when an order is cancelled before shipment, the customer was double-charged, or the item is confirmed lost, damaged, or materially different from the listing.

**RF-2 Original Payment Method.** Refunds must be returned to the original payment method unless Finance has approved an alternate method.

**RF-3 Double Charges.** Confirmed duplicate charges should be refunded for the duplicate amount only. Agents should cite both charge IDs in the case note.

**RF-4 Approval Threshold.** Refunds over $50 require human approval before the customer is promised a refund.

**GW-1 Goodwill Use.** A goodwill credit may be offered for service failures such as carrier delays, missed delivery promises, or repeated contact.

**GW-2 Goodwill Cap.** Agents may issue goodwill credits up to $10 per order without approval.

**GW-3 No Cash Substitution.** Goodwill credits are account credits and must not be described as cash refunds.

**RF-5 Documentation.** Every refund or goodwill credit must include the order ID, reason, amount, and policy clause in the audit note.
